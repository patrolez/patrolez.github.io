amfh265enc

amfh265enc element encodes raw video stream into compressed H.265 bitstream via AMD AMF API.

Example launch line

gst-launch-1.0 videotestsrc num-buffers=100 ! amfh265enc ! h265parse ! mp4mux ! filesink location=encoded.mp4
