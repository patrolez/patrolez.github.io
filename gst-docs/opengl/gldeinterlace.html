gldeinterlace

Deinterlace video using OpenGL fragment shaders.

Examples

 gst-launch-1.0 videotestsrc ! glupload ! gldeinterlace ! glimagesink

FBO (Frame Buffer Object) and GLSL (OpenGL Shading Language) are required.
