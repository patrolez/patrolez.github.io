vaapipostproc

vaapipostproc consists in various postprocessing algorithms to be applied to VA surfaces.

Example launch line

 gst-launch-1.0 videotestsrc ! vaapipostproc ! video/x-raw, width=1920, height=1080 ! vaapisink
