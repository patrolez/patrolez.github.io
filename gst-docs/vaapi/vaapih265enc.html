vaapih265enc

Encodes raw video streams into HEVC bitstreams.

Example launch line

  gst-launch-1.0 -ev videotestsrc num-buffers=60 ! timeoverlay ! vaapih265enc ! h265parse ! matroskamux ! filesink location=test.mkv
