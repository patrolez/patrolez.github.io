Members

momentary (0x00000001) – Calculate momentary loudness (400ms)
short-term (0x00000002) – Calculate short-term loudness (3s)
global (0x00000004) – Calculate relative threshold and global loudness
loudness-range (0x00000008) – Calculate loudness range
sample-peak (0x00000010) – Calculate sample peak
true-peak (0x00000020) – Calculate true peak
